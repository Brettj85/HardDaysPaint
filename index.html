About Us
Hello, first we would like to thank you for taking the time consider A hard Day’s Paint. We firmly believe that the environment of your home has a great impact on your state of mind. We take much pride and honor in being a part of your positive transformation. We provide quality services done by experienced painters. I have been in the painting business for 10 years and decided to make my own company 5 years ago. I found a partner who shared my integrity, attention to detail, and beliefs on how important customer service is. We treat every job like it’s our family and show it the attention and respect it deserves. We are neat, clean and professional working with only premium high-quality paints and stains. We offer a guarantee on all our work, and we strive to meet the satisfaction of all our clients. Give us a call anytime and we will come back for touch ups or repair free of charge. If you have any questions or concerns feel free to reach out to us at any time by text, phone or email, we look forward to “working like a dog so you don’t have to!”
